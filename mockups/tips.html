Tips & Grammar Guide

Respectful Greetings

In Bemba, respect is very important. When speaking to someone older or in a position of service (like a vendor), we use the plural form.

Singular vs. Plural (Respect)

Uli shani? (Informal/Singular)

Used with friends or children.

Muli shani? (Formal/Plural)

Used with elders, strangers, or asking a group.

The Prefix "Mu-"

You'll notice the prefix Mu- adds "You (plural)" or "In/Inside". Here, it turns "Uli" (You are) into "Muli" (You derive respect).

Cultural Insight

When initiating a purchase at a market (Musika), it is polite to greet first before asking for the price. Skipping the greeting can be seen as rude!
